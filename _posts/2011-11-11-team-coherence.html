--- layout: post title: Team Coherence post_time: November 11 2011, 9:58 PM ---

When a piece of technology appears to be the work of a single mind and possesses the dense layers of coherent complexity that can only be the product of a large team, it is evidence of a deep coherence in the team itself. In such a team, individuals trust the collective to the point that  they feel comfortable narrowing their domain of conscious concern to their own work.

Venkat, Technology and the Baroque Unconscious
